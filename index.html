Connect Instagram

To post for you, we need your permission. Here's exactly what happens:

  1. You authorize us below.
  2. We text and email you a secure link. Tap it, then connect your accounts - you approve on the platform's own page, and we never see your password.
  3. Once it's connected, we post your approved videos automatically.

First: make your Instagram a Professional account

Instagram only allows automation and posting on Professional accounts (Business or Creator). This is required before we connect your account here or run Social Autopilot. It's free, takes about 2 minutes, and you can switch back any time.

  1. Open the Instagram app, go to your profile, and tap the menu (☰) → Settings and privacy.
  2. Find Account type and tools (sometimes shown under "For professionals"), then tap Switch to professional account.
  3. Choose a category, then pick Business (best for most local businesses) or Creator, and tap Next.
  4. When prompted, connect your Facebook Page - this is needed for full automation. No Page yet? Create a free one first; Instagram's business tools connect through it.

Official Instagram guide: help.instagram.com
